Maxfiylik Siyosati

Oxirgi yangilanish: 16 yanvar 2026

1. Kirish

GreenMarket ilovasi foydalanuvchilarning maxfiyligini himoya qilishga sodiqdir. Ushbu maxfiylik siyosati ilovamiz tomonidan qanday ma'lumotlar to'planishi, ishlatilishi va himoya qilinishini tushuntiradi.

2. To'planadigan Ma'lumotlar

GreenMarket ilovasi quyidagi ma'lumotlarni to'playdi:

  • Qurilma ma'lumotlari: Ilova versiyasi, qurilma modeli, operatsion tizim versiyasi (faqat texnik xizmat ko'rsatish uchun)
  • Foydalanuvchi sozlamalari: Til tanlovi, bildirishnoma sozlamalari (faqat mahalliy qurilmada saqlanadi)
  • Savatcha ma'lumotlari: Tanlangan mahsulotlar va miqdori (faqat mahalliy qurilmada saqlanadi)
  • Yoqtirgan mahsulotlar: Sevimli mahsulotlar ro'yxati (faqat mahalliy qurilmada saqlanadi)

3. Ma'lumotlardan Foydalanish

To'plangan ma'lumotlar quyidagi maqsadlarda ishlatiladi:

  • Ilova funksiyalarini ta'minlash
  • Foydalanuvchi tajribasini yaxshilash
  • Texnik muammolarni hal qilish
  • Yangilanishlar haqida xabardor qilish

4. Ma'lumotlarni Saqlash

Barcha shaxsiy ma'lumotlar faqat foydalanuvchining qurilmasida mahalliy saqlanadi. Biz serverlarimizda shaxsiy ma'lumotlarni saqlamaymiz.

5. Uchinchi Tomon Xizmatlari

Ilovamiz quyidagi uchinchi tomon xizmatlaridan foydalanadi:

  • Google AdMob: Reklama ko'rsatish uchun. AdMob o'z maxfiylik siyosatiga ega: Google Privacy Policy
  • Vercel API: Mahsulot ma'lumotlari va bog'bonchilik maslahatlari uchun

6. Bolalar Maxfiyligi

Ilovamiz 13 yoshdan kichik bolalardan ongli ravishda shaxsiy ma'lumot to'plamaydi. Agar siz ota-ona yoki vasiy bo'lsangiz va farzandingiz bizga shaxsiy ma'lumot berganini bilsangiz, biz bilan bog'laning.

7. Ma'lumotlar Xavfsizligi

Biz foydalanuvchi ma'lumotlarini himoya qilish uchun quyidagi choralarni ko'ramiz:

  • Kod obfuscation (shifrlash)
  • Ilova yaxlitligini tekshirish
  • Xavfsiz API aloqalari (HTTPS)
  • Mahalliy ma'lumotlarni shifrlash

8. Foydalanuvchi Huquqlari

Siz quyidagi huquqlarga egasiz:

  • O'z ma'lumotlaringizga kirish
  • Ma'lumotlarni o'chirish (ilovani o'chirish orqali)
  • Bildirishnomalarni o'chirish/yoqish
  • Reklama sozlamalarini boshqarish

9. Siyosat O'zgarishlari

Biz ushbu maxfiylik siyosatini vaqti-vaqti bilan yangilashimiz mumkin. Barcha o'zgarishlar ushbu sahifada e'lon qilinadi va ilova orqali foydalanuvchilarga xabar beriladi.

10. Aloqa

Agar maxfiylik siyosati haqida savollaringiz bo'lsa, biz bilan bog'laning:

Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: January 16, 2026

1. Introduction

GreenMarket app is committed to protecting user privacy. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, used, and protected by our app.

2. Data Collection

GreenMarket collects the following data:

  • Device information: App version, device model, OS version (for technical support only)
  • User preferences: Language selection, notification settings (stored locally only)
  • Cart data: Selected products and quantities (stored locally only)
  • Liked products: Favorite products list (stored locally only)

3. Data Usage

Collected data is used for:

  • Providing app functionality
  • Improving user experience
  • Resolving technical issues
  • Notifying about updates

4. Data Storage

All personal data is stored locally on the user's device only. We do not store personal data on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying ads. AdMob has its own privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Vercel API: For product data and gardening advice

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect user data:

  • Code obfuscation
  • App integrity verification
  • Secure API communications (HTTPS)
  • Local data encryption

8. User Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access your data
  • Delete data (by uninstalling the app)
  • Disable/enable notifications
  • Manage ad settings

9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page and users will be notified through the app.
